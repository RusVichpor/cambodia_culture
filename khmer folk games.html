Khmer Folk Games

លោតបាវ(Jumping over the Rice / Bamboo Stick)

Players jump over a moving rice bundle or bamboo stick that is gradually raised; tests agility, timing, and flexibility.

វាយក្អម ( Hit the Can / Object Game)

Hit a target object with a stick; tests aim, skill, and accuracy.

បោះអង្គាញ់(Knucklebones / Tossing Bones)

Players toss small bones, stones, or objects according to rules; improves dexterity, counting skills, and concentration.

លាក់កន្សែង(Hide the Scarf )

One person hides a scarf behind others, and players try to find it; develops observation and quick reflexes.

ទាញព្រ័ត្រ(Tug of War )

Two teams pull a rope against each other; tests strength and teamwork.

បូមទឹកក្នុងមាត់(Mouth Water Sucking / Sponge Game)

Players use their mouth to suck water from a container and transfer it to another; fun water game, builds laughter and teamwork.
